📰 News


News is the way we learn about what is happening around us – in our city, country, and the world:


  • 🏛️ Politics – government decisions, elections, and leaders.
  • 🏥 Health – new diseases, treatments, and health tips.
  • 📈 Economy – jobs, money, prices, and business news.
  • 🌍 World events – important things happening in other countries.
  • 🎭 Entertainment – movies, celebrities, and shows.
  • ⚽ Sports – matches, scores, and player updates.

📢 Why is News Important?

  • 🧠 Helps people stay informed and aware.
  • 🗳️ Helps citizens make smart decisions, like voting.
  • 👥 Connects people to the world and to each other.
  • 🔍 Allows people to learn the truth and fight fake news.



🌦️ Weather

Weather means the condition of the air outside at a certain place and time. It includes things like:

  • ☀️ Sunshine – when the sun is shining and the sky is clear.
  • 🌧️ Rain – when water falls from the sky as droplets.
  • ⛅ Clouds – when the sky is covered with white or grey clouds.
  • 🌬️ Wind – when air moves across the land.
  • ❄️ Snow – when frozen flakes fall in cold weather.
  • 🌡️ Temperature – how hot or cold it is.
  • 🌩️ Storms – when there is thunder, lightning, and heavy rain.

📅 Why we want to know about Weather?

  • 🌄 Helps people decide what to wear.
  • 🧑‍🌾 Farmers use it to plan farming work.
  • 🛫 Important for safe travel.
  • 🎉 Useful for planning events like weddings or trips.
